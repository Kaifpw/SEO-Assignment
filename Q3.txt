Meta tags are a critical part of search engine optimization (SEO). 
They provide concise information about a web page's content, helping search engines understand what the page is about.
 Meta tags can also help improve a webpage's click-through rate by providing more detailed information about the content of the page.

 Some of the main benefits:
 
 1)Providing relevancy
 2)Helping search engines determine the topic of a webpage
 3)Helping people understand the contents of a webpage
 4)Avoiding duplicate content issues
 5)Optimizing a website's content for specific keywords and phrases
 6)Improving search engine visibility
 7)Impacting how a site appears in the SERPs
 8)Impacting traffic and engagement rates
 9)Impacting SEO and rankings
